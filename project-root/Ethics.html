Ethics in using Ai

- Responsible use in the academic activities

AI should only be used as a tool for educational purposes. specific programs like scispace.com or Turbo.ai may be used as to summarize research papers or note taking.
Blatant use of AI in a manner of plagerism and copy pasting is admitted.

- Avoiding plagiarism and misuse

Avoid making use of AI to do work for you. You only hinder yourself with this unadvisable use of Artificial intellegence.

- Accuracy and verification of AI outputs

To verify outputs people use AI checker like for example quilbot.

- Bias and fairness

AI can be bias with their outputs so be careful on how you use them. They may persuade you to change your position.
